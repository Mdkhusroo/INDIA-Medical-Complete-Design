Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a prescription to order medicines? expand_more
For prescription medicines, a valid prescription from a registered medical practitioner is required as per regulations. For over-the-counter (OTC) products, no prescription is needed.
What payment methods are available? expand_more
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) and various online payment methods including UPI and bank transfers. Our team will guide you through the options when you place your order.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it? expand_more
Yes, you can modify or cancel your order, provided it has not yet been dispatched for delivery. Please contact our customer support immediately to make changes.
What if I can't find a specific medicine? expand_more
Please contact us directly! Even if a medicine isn't listed, we might be able to source it for you or suggest an appropriate alternative.
