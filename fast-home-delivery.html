Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours? expand_more
Our delivery services operate from 9 AM to 9 PM, seven days a week.
Is there a minimum order value for delivery? expand_more
Yes, a minimum order value of ₹200 applies for home delivery services. However, delivery is **free** for orders within 3 kilometers of our location!
What areas do you deliver to? expand_more
We currently offer delivery services across Gulbarga, Karnataka. Please contact us for specific area coverage details.
How can I track my order? expand_more
Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an update via WhatsApp. You can also call our customer support for real-time tracking.
